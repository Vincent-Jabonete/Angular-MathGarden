About Us

The application contains four different chapters with two to four different lessons each. Every chapter contains a chapter quiz composed of thirty random questions to assess the users’ progress. The chapter quiz question will come from the lesson that the chapter contains. Every lesson contains a video lesson and quizzes compose of ten random questions. The user will be required to finish the video before proceeding to the lesson quiz. In the lesson quiz, the user needs to get at least a 50% score or higher to pass and to proceed or to unlock the next lesson and if the user unlocked all the lessons and get a passing score they will now proceed to the chapter quiz.

In the chapter quiz, if the user will pass the chapter, he/she will get a certificate as a reward for completing the chapter. The certificate the user will get can be viewed in the achievements and can be saved in the users’ mobile phone. There’s also Achievements and Nursery Rhyme. In achievements, this is where the user will see all their scores in every quiz, certificates unlocked and lessons unlocked in the app. While in the nursery rhyme this is where the user will see four different nursery rhymes that are related in four chapters the application contains.
